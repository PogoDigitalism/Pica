Manage your government services with Texas by Texas (TxT).
TxT is your digital assistant making government services easier, faster, and more secure.

With TxT you can:

Create a universal login and password to access government services across multiple agencies
Complete services like vehicles registration renewals, driver license and ID card renewals and replacements, and more
Get timely alerts for important due dates
Manage your account on any device from your TxT dashboard
